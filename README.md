# 🎭 OOZE: The Character Crucible

## Overview

OOZE is an experimental multi-agent sandbox that transforms stagnant decision-making into unscripted theatrical debate. Built for the "Play" Hackathon, OOZE rejects the standard "agreeable" AI model in favor of a friction-based simulation. By treating "The Persona as DNA," OOZE allows users to cast a "Council" of clashing archetypes who collide, argue, and mutate their logic to stress-test your real-world dilemmas.

## The Problem

Current AI is designed to be agreeable, which is the enemy of breakthrough thinking. Most LLMs are tuned for neutrality and politeness, leading to "middle-of-the-road" advice that fails to provide clarity during high-stakes moments. When you are at a crossroads, a helpful assistant that simply agrees with you is a liability. Agreeableness kills innovation and indecision thrives in a vacuum of conflict.

## The Solution

OOZE is a decision-support tool that uses conflict as a feature. Instead of a single chatbot, you assemble a "Council" of clashing agents. By dropping your dilemma into this "Semantic Sandbox," you trigger an unscripted debate where agents collide and mutate their logic through "semantic physics." You don't just get an answer; you watch a simulation of the world's most intense perspectives fighting to solve your problem.

## Who It's For

The Over-Analyzers: People facing high-stakes life decisions who want to see their dilemma "stress-tested" by extreme archetypes.

The Creative Block-Breakers: Writers and designers who need a "collision" of perspectives to shake up stagnant projects.

The Leadership "Sim" Market: Managers practicing tough conversations by running them through a "Council" of history's most difficult personalities.

## Why OOZE? (The Edge)

While other AI apps focus on accuracy and alignment, OOZE focuses on friction and perspective.

The Spectrum of Truth: We don't give you a "best" answer; we give you the full range of possibilities.

Theatrical Utility: It turns dry prompt engineering into a tactile, living toy—making the decision process visually hypnotic and intellectually rigorous.

## The Bottom Line

Stop asking the AI what to do. Start watching the AI fight about it. OOZE transforms decision-making from a lonely internal monologue into a theatrical "Character Crucible," ensuring your eventual path has survived the world's toughest critics.

## Development

Stack: React (Vite), Express API, Tailwind CSS.

```bash
npm install
npm run dev
```

Runs the Vite dev server and `server.js` together. Use `npm run build` for production assets and `npm run preview` to preview the build.
